About Brainadet

Brainadet soccer foundation is an independent soccer and cognitve develoment charity. The foundation is held by guarantee with qualified soccer instructors and alied educatorsto nurturing identified raw raw soccer talents within it operative environs and boyound to becoming skilful and fully-fledge professionals, in order to integrate into the global soccer economy.

Brainadet soccer foundation was formed by two soccer loving fans namely Kaiser Julius Briandt and Joseph Kofi Adeti who is a license coach. The foundation started in the year 2015 but was duly registered in the year 2018. The foundation's mission is to help mitigate talent abuse, streetism and unhealthy peer cicles abuse and extinction, and our vission is to become a world class educational and soccer academy. Since its formation we have participated in a number of tournaments within the municipality and all we did so well. Education being a core part of the foundation, we make time to visit our players in their various schools to interact with with their teachers to know how they are performing. The foundation has teams ranging from under 8,10,13,15, and 17all together numbering about 80 kids/players
